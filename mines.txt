MINES

Goals:
- Walk the longest distances (highest number of steps) in the mine field (1 point per step)
- Collect $ bonus items (5 extra points + 1 extra "escape" if the maximum of 3 is not reached)
- End the game by bumping on a mine (0 points) or reaching the top or bottom border (50 bonus points)
- Try to reach the highest record

Controls:
- Use W A S D to move 
- Use SPACE to clear nearby area (max 3 times + extra escapes collected with $)
